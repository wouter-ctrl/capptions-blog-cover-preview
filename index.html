Capptions · blog cover system

Six looping covers, generated once, zero cropped photos.

Why a generated loop, not a photo. No source image ever needs cropping, licensing, or sourcing per post. Six premium motion loops, generated once, reused across every article, so per-post cost stays at zero.
Palette. Capptions' real tokens: teal (hsl 174 100% 35%) as the accent, white background, matching the live site, not the Seveso landing page's own theme.

Case studies get their own format

Currently every customer story is just another blog post, same template, same generated cover, no way to tell "we already wrote about Ardagh" from a fresh article. That's structural, not a content problem: it's why the same case study kept reappearing under new slugs. Splitting it into its own type fixes that at the source.

Rotation logic

Each post picks its motif from hash(slug) % 6, so a given article always renders the same loop on rebuild, but the sequence across the blog index feels varied rather than repetitive.

Build notes

Six .mp4 loops, generated once via Higgsfield, reused across every post, not one per article. Respects prefers-reduced-motion (loads a frame, then freezes instead of playing). Same six loops drive both the blog index card and the post detail hero, and the case-study lockup on top.
